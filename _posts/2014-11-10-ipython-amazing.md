I wanted to look up
all the top-level, grid related functions
available in NumPy.
On the IPython shell,
on a whim,
I typed:

    np.*grid*?

not knowing what to expect.
It did just what I wanted:

    np.meshgrid
    np.mgrid
    np.ogrid

That made my day.
Thanks, IPython!
